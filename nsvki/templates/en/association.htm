The NSVKI is the cooperation for Study Associations for Artificial Intelligence in the Netherlands. The members VIA (Amsterdam), STORM (Amsterdam), Cover (Groningen), CognAC (Nijmegen) and USCKI Incognito (Utrecht) decided it to be a good idea to keep in contact in this form. Our aim is to support Dutch AI students, and foster ties between students in all of the Netherlands.
